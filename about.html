About Connected Earth

Connected Earth is an interactive platform designed to explore the intricate relationships between our planet’s natural systems — from rainfall and droughts to wildfires, pollution, and human health.

Our goal is to make climate science engaging, educational, and accessible. Using immersive visuals and storytelling, we show how one change in the environment can ripple across ecosystems, societies, and ultimately affect us all.

This project draws inspiration from NASA’s Earth-observing satellites, which provide critical insights into these connections. By blending technology and design, we aim to inspire awareness and encourage collective action toward a sustainable future.
