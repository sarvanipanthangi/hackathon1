Discover the Connections

Our Earth systems are intricately connected on a global scale. Climate change can increase the length and severity of droughts, which can make wildfires more prevalent. These fires worsen air quality and disrupt ecosystems, but they also influence forest regeneration and biodiversity.

NASA’s Earth observing satellites provide critical insights into these connections, allowing us to study how changes in one system ripple through others. By blending science, technology, and storytelling, we can communicate these relationships in ways that are immersive, educational, and visually engaging.
