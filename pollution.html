Pollution & Climate

Pollution comes from industries, vehicles, and even wildfires. Smoke and greenhouse gases trap heat in the atmosphere, driving climate change. Fine particles from burning forests or factories pollute the air, causing respiratory issues and reducing overall health. Tackling pollution is essential to protect ecosystems and create a more sustainable, healthy planet.
