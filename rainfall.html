Rainfall & Climate

Rainfall plays a vital role in regulating our climate and sustaining ecosystems. When rainfall levels are unusually low for extended periods, it can lead to droughts. This lack of water not only impacts agriculture and drinking water supplies but also worsens wildfires, affects air quality, and disrupts biodiversity.
