🩺 Impact on Human Health

The effects of environmental changes—from reduced rainfall to pollution—ultimately impact human health. Air pollution contributes to respiratory and cardiovascular diseases, while extreme heat and water shortages threaten vulnerable populations. It's essential to monitor environmental indicators to anticipate and reduce the risks to human well-being. Climate-related health challenges are a growing concern and require coordinated global action.
